about us

The main goal of the Lviv Smart City - to inspire leaders and agents of changes in our city. To give them maximum opportunities to implement their own projects, which as a result, will contribute to the development of Lviv and West Ukraine.
We see Lviv as a European city that Ukrainian and international companies from the information technologies sphere select for their effective development of business. Lviv is a city with the highest quality of education in Ukraine, the city of comfortable life, professional growth opportunities, city of new technologies development and progressive ideas realization.
